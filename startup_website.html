Neil deGrasse Tyson

Neil deGrasse Tyson is a world-renowned astrophysicist and science communicator who brings the wonders of the universe to life. As director of the Hayden Planetarium, he inspires curiosity about the cosmos through his engaging storytelling and charismatic presence. Known for Cosmos and StarTalk, he makes complex science accessible and exciting for everyone. A passionate advocate for education and critical thinking, Tyson sparks a love for discovery in audiences worldwide. His work reminds us to look up and marvel at the universe.
